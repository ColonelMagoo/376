Smash Brothers?

Index Origins

Waluigi's presence in Smash Brothers has long been a controversy from fans of the series. He has been involved in the game but he only appears as an NPC. Any hope of him being playable in the upcoming game on the Switch has now been dashed as the complete roster has been announced and sadly doesn't include him.

Waluigi appeared in Super Smash Bros. Brawl on the Wii as an assist trophy that would stomp players into the ground.
